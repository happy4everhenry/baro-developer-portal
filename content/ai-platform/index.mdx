# AI Platform

<PageMeta status="in_progress" layer="backend" />

The brain behind BARO — 12 specialized AI agents, a 7-layer technology stack, and a governance framework that keeps everything safe, fair, and auditable. Architecture is complete and fully specified. Phase 1 agents (Risk Guard, Smart Notification, Security Monitor, Control Tower) are now in active development.

<InfoBox type="fun" title="Think of it like a team of robots!">
  Imagine 12 smart robots, each with one specific job. One watches stock prices, another reads the news, another guards against fraud. They all work together, but a human always makes the final decision on anything involving money.
</InfoBox>
