## Phase Breakdown

<Tabs>
  <Tab label="Phase 1" icon="🏗️">
    ### Foundation <StatusBadge status="live">Complete</StatusBadge>

    The basics that every stock app needs. All screens built and shipped!

    - **Home Screen** — Your launchpad with watchlist and market summary
    - **Stock Detail** — Deep dive into any stock
    - **Market Overview** — The big picture of the whole market
    - **Search** — Find any stock instantly

    These four screens form the core of BARO. Everything else builds on top of them.
  </Tab>
  <Tab label="Phase 2" icon="🚀">
    ### Expansion <StatusBadge status="live">Complete</StatusBadge>

    More features, more power, more fun. All screens shipped!

    - **Login & Authentication** — Secure user accounts and session management
    - **Events & Calendar** — Market events, earnings, dividends, and IPOs
    - **MyPage & Settings** — Personal profile, portfolio, and preferences
    - **Notifications** — Real-time alerts for prices, events, and AI insights
    - **AI on Home** — Intelligence Hub merged into the Home screen's AI section

    BARO has gone from "cool stock viewer" to "full AI investing companion."
  </Tab>
  <Tab label="Phase 3" icon="🧠">
    ### Intelligence <StatusBadge status="in-progress">In Progress</StatusBadge>

    The big, ambitious, exciting stuff. Architecture defined — implementation starting!

    - **12 AI Agents** — Each one a specialist (chart reader, news summarizer, trend spotter, and more)
    - **Voice Assistant** — Talk to BARO like you talk to Siri
    - **RAG Pipeline** — AI that reads thousands of documents to answer your questions

    This is the future — and it's being built right now.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<InfoBox type="tip" title="The AI Vision">
Most stock apps just show you numbers. BARO wants to **explain** those numbers. Imagine asking "Why did Samsung go up today?" and getting a clear, simple answer that even a kid could understand. That's what the 12 AI agents are being built to do — each one specializing in a different type of analysis, working together like a team of super-smart analysts.
</InfoBox>
