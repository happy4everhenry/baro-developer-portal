Development timeline and planned features for BARO.

## Phase 1 --- Foundation <StatusBadge status="live">COMPLETE</StatusBadge>

Core iOS app: Home dashboard, stock list, market indices, news, search, basic navigation.

**Completed:** Q1–Q2 2025

## Phase 2 --- Stock Analysis <StatusBadge status="live">COMPLETE</StatusBadge>

Stock detail page: interactive chart (23 types, 155+ indicators), order book, daily prices, financials, dividends, analyst opinions, investor flow.

**Completed:** Q3 2025

## Phase 3 --- Market Intelligence <StatusBadge status="live">COMPLETE</StatusBadge>

AI market issues bubble chart, detail sheets (reports, dividends, financials with quarterly/annual toggle).

**Completed:** Q3 2025

## Phase 4 --- Backend Integration <StatusBadge status="live">COMPLETE</StatusBadge>

FastAPI backend with dual data sources (Yahoo Finance + KIS Open API). REST endpoints for stock quotes, charts, orderbooks, rankings. Real-time WebSocket streaming with KIS relay, subscription fan-out, and animated price ticking on iOS.

**Completed:** Q4 2025

## Phase 5 --- Expansion (Login, Events, MyPage, Notifications) <StatusBadge status="live">COMPLETE</StatusBadge>

Login & authentication (100%), Events & calendar (100%), MyPage & settings (100%), Notifications (100%). Intelligence Hub merged into Home screen's AI section. Full backend specification finalized.

**Completed:** Q1 2026

**Progress:** Architecture complete, implementation starting (March 2026)
