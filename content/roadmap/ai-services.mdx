## 12 AI Agents

### Phase 1 — In Development (Q2 2026)

<InfoBox type="info" title="Implementation Starting">
These four agents form the foundation of the AI platform. Architecture defined, development beginning.
</InfoBox>

#### Risk Guard <StatusBadge status="in-progress">In Development</StatusBadge>

Real-time position risk monitoring, portfolio stress testing, regulatory compliance checks (FSC), and anomaly detection. Protects users from excessive risk exposure.

#### Smart Notification <StatusBadge status="in-progress">In Development</StatusBadge>

Intelligent alert system that prioritizes and personalizes notifications based on user behavior, portfolio holdings, and market conditions. Reduces noise, surfaces what matters.

#### Security Monitor <StatusBadge status="in-progress">In Development</StatusBadge>

Continuous security surveillance across the platform — detects prompt injection attempts, data poisoning, anomalous API patterns, and unauthorized access. The AI platform's immune system.

#### Control Tower <StatusBadge status="in-progress">In Development</StatusBadge>

Orchestration agent that coordinates all other agents, manages task routing, handles fallbacks, and ensures system-wide consistency. The brain that keeps the 12-agent team working together.

### Phase 2 — Specified (Q3 2026)

<InfoBox type="info" title="Fully Specified">
These agents have complete specifications and will enter development after Phase 1 agents are operational.
</InfoBox>

#### Market Analyst <StatusBadge status="planned">Specified</StatusBadge>

AI-powered market analysis combining technical indicators (155+), sector rotation detection, and macro trend identification. Provides daily market briefs and real-time commentary.

#### Portfolio Advisor <StatusBadge status="planned">Specified</StatusBadge>

Personalized investment advisor with chat interface, portfolio analysis, rebalancing suggestions, and trade recommendations based on user risk profile and goals.

#### News Analyst <StatusBadge status="planned">Specified</StatusBadge>

Real-time financial news processing — summarizes articles, extracts key events, identifies affected stocks, and assesses sentiment impact on holdings.

#### Sentiment Tracker <StatusBadge status="planned">Specified</StatusBadge>

Real-time market sentiment analysis from news, social media, and financial reports using NLP and local LLM inference. Tracks crowd mood shifts before they hit prices.

### Phase 3 — Planned (Q4 2026)

<InfoBox type="info" title="Planned">
These agents are designed and will be built after Phase 2 agents are operational.
</InfoBox>

#### Trading Strategist <StatusBadge status="planned">Planned</StatusBadge>

Technical analysis signal generation with buy/sell/hold recommendations, backtested strategy templates, and automated execution planning.

#### Compliance Auditor <StatusBadge status="planned">Planned</StatusBadge>

Regulatory compliance automation — FSC rule checking, KYC verification support, trade surveillance, and audit report generation.

#### Voice Assistant <StatusBadge status="planned">Planned</StatusBadge>

Natural language voice interface for BARO. Ask questions about stocks, get portfolio updates, and execute commands using spoken Korean and English.

#### Document Analyst <StatusBadge status="planned">Planned</StatusBadge>

RAG-powered financial document Q&A — reads and understands earnings calls, annual reports, regulatory filings, and research papers to answer complex questions.
