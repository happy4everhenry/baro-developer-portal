<InfoBox type="fun" title="What is a splash screen?">
A **splash screen** is the very first thing you see when you open the app — like the cover of a book. It shows the BARO logo while the app prepares everything behind the scenes.
</InfoBox>

## What It Does

The splash screen runs for 2–3 seconds and performs two important tasks:

1. **Shows the brand** — Displays the BARO logo with an animated progress bar
2. **Checks authentication** — Reads the stored JWT token from Keychain to determine if the user is already logged in
