<InfoBox type="fun" title="What are notifications?">
**Notifications** keep you in the loop — like a friend who taps your shoulder to say "Hey, Samsung just hit your target price!" They make sure you never miss important market events.
</InfoBox>

### Notification Settings

| Category | Toggle Options |
|----------|---------------|
| Price Alerts | Target price reached, daily summary |
| News | Breaking news, analyst reports |
| Events | New events, event reminders |
| Marketing | Promotions, product updates |

## Features

- **Read/unread state** — Visual indicator for new notifications
- **Category-based** — Price alerts, news, events, and marketing
- **Customizable preferences** — Toggle each category on/off
- **Tap to navigate** — Price alerts open stock detail, news opens article
