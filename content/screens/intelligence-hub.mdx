AI-powered investment intelligence with structured reports, market analysis, and personalized recommendations.

<InfoBox type="info" title="Intelligence Hub — Live">
The Intelligence Hub is BARO's AI-powered research center, shipped as part of Phase 2. It presents structured analysis reports with visual sections — from key metrics to bull/bear analysis, timelines, and actionable recommendations. The base UI and report viewing features are live. AI-powered agent features (chatbot, real-time signals, voice assistant) are planned for Phase 3.
</InfoBox>

## What Is the Intelligence Hub?

Think of it as your personal research assistant. The Intelligence Hub combines a powerful universal search bar with curated intelligence cards, giving you everything you need to understand what the market is doing right now.
