# News Detail <KoreanLabel>뉴스 상세</KoreanLabel>

<PageMeta status="live" layer="frontend" since="2025" />

<InfoBox type="fun" title="What is the news detail screen?">
When you tap a news article on the home screen, you see its **full story** here — like opening a newspaper article to read the whole thing.
</InfoBox>

## What You See

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  KLDetailNavBar             │  ← Back button + title
│  Article Title              │  ← Headline text
│  Source + Date              │  ← "한국경제 · 30분 전"
│  AI Summary                 │  ← AI-generated 3-line summary
│  Full Content               │  ← Article body text
│  Tags                       │  ← #삼성전자 #HBM
│  Related Stocks             │  ← Tappable stock chips
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

## Features

- **AI-generated summary** — 3-line digest at the top for quick reading
- **Source attribution** — Publisher name and relative time
- **Category & region badges** — Market, stocks, themes, economy, etc.
- **Content type** — News, analyst reports, or regulatory disclosures
- **Related stock links** — Tap to navigate to stock detail
- **Hashtag tags** — Topic tags for the article
