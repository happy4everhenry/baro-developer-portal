# What is BARO?

<InfoBox type="fun" title="Think of it like this...">
Imagine you have a super-smart piggy bank app on your phone. But instead of just saving coins, it uses AI to help you invest wisely — picking stocks, tracking your portfolio, and guiding you with smart recommendations. That's BARO!
</InfoBox>

BARO is a **Korean AI robo-advisor** that helps people invest in stocks, ETFs, and currencies — all from their iPhone, guided by intelligent recommendations. It's built with **SwiftUI**, Apple's newest way to make beautiful apps. Development is organized into three phases: **Phase 1** (core screens and market data) and **Phase 2** (stock detail, search, authentication) are complete. **Phase 3** (AI-powered insights and recommendations) is currently in progress.

## How the App is Built

Think of BARO like a three-story building. The **iOS app** is the top floor (what you see), the **Python backend** is the middle floor (processes data and talks to stock exchanges), and the **AI platform** is the smart basement (analyzes everything and makes recommendations).

<InfoBox type="info" title="What does each layer do?">
- **Screens** — The pretty part you see! Buttons, charts, colors. Built with SwiftUI.
- **ViewModels** — The brain. Takes data and decides what to show you.
- **Repositories** — Like a librarian. Knows where to find all the information.
- **FastAPI Backend** — The server that processes requests, handles login, and relays live stock prices from Korean exchanges.
- **KIS API** — Korea Investment & Securities — the actual stock exchange connection for real-time prices, charts, and order books.
- **AI Agents** — 12 specialized AI helpers that analyze markets, detect risks, generate reports, and power the chatbot. Currently being built (Phase 3).
</InfoBox>
