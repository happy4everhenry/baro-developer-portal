# What is BARO?

<InfoBox type="fun" title="Think of it like this...">
Imagine you have a super-smart piggy bank app on your phone. But instead of just saving coins, it uses AI to help you invest wisely — picking stocks, tracking your portfolio, and guiding you with smart recommendations. That's BARO!
</InfoBox>

BARO is a **Korean AI robo-advisor** that helps people invest in stocks, ETFs, and currencies — all from their iPhone, guided by intelligent recommendations. It's built with **SwiftUI**, Apple's newest way to make beautiful apps. Development is organized into three phases: **Phase 1** (core screens and market data) and **Phase 2** (stock detail, search, authentication) are complete. **Phase 3** (AI-powered insights and recommendations) is currently in progress.

## How the App is Built

Think of BARO like a layered cake. Each layer has a special job:

<Diagram title="How BARO Works (Layer Cake!)">
  <DiagramRow>
    <DiagramNode type="view" icon="📱" label="Screen" sublabel="What you see and tap" />
  </DiagramRow>
  <DiagramArrow direction="down" label="talks to" />
  <DiagramRow>
    <DiagramNode type="vm" icon="🧠" label="ViewModel" sublabel="The brain that thinks" />
  </DiagramRow>
  <DiagramArrow direction="down" label="asks for data" />
  <DiagramRow>
    <DiagramNode type="repo" icon="📦" label="Repository" sublabel="The storage room" />
  </DiagramRow>
  <DiagramArrow direction="down" label="fetches from" />
  <DiagramRow>
    <DiagramNode type="service" icon="🌐" label="API" sublabel="The internet connection" />
  </DiagramRow>
</Diagram>

<InfoBox type="info" title="What does each layer do?">
- **Screen** — The pretty part you see! Buttons, charts, colors.
- **ViewModel** — The brain. It takes data and decides what to show you.
- **Repository** — Like a librarian. It knows where to find all the information.
- **API** — The messenger that talks to servers on the internet to get live prices.
</InfoBox>
